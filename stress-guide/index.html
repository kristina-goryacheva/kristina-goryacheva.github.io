Stress Guide

Personal stress coach right in your pocket

About the Stress Guide

Duration:
2018-2020
Role:
Product Designer, Illustrator
Platform:
IOS & Android apps

The Stress Guide mobile application educates the users about their stress level and helps them to learn to manage it. The app measures stress index and relaxation ability using just a smartphone camera and provides a variety of stress coping methods including: in-depths courses, meditations, sounds and breathing exercises.

I helped to design a new version of Stress Guide. Together with Kenkou team we rethough the user experience, introduced new features and completely changed the visual design of the app.

App StoreGoogle play

Engaging onboarding

The challenge was to design an easy-to-use and engaging application. We simplified the flow of the app, made onboarding more engaging by giving the ability to set the primary goal for the user. By doing this we improved the personalized experience, making sure the users get the relevant and valuable content.

Home screen

The home screen offers direct access to the stress measurement, history, mindfulness courses, exercices and personal progress. By completing exercises and measurements users see the changes in personal progress which directly leads to a positive habit forming.

Stress measurement

The key feature of the Stress Guide app - measurement allows the user to track stress index, recovery ability, as well as pulse and mood. We tried to make the measurement flow easy and understandable, and simplified the measurement result so that the users are not confused by overly complicated scientific data.

Guide: mindfulness courses and exercises

The Guide part of the app contains stress-related courses and exercises are made to help reduce user’s stress and improve overall well being. Whether through a guided meditation, a deep breathing exercise or a life-changing tip.

We created a library of over 100 relaxing guided meditations for our users. By reducing stress and anxiety user’s also form a positive healthy habit of consistent meditating.

The positive effects of breathing exercises have long been known and proven. Stress Guide provides proven breathing exercises technique with custom calming illustrations to help the users to prevent burnout, increase heart health and improve sleep.

The impact and positive results

When we started to work on the Stress Guide, the app struggled to meet user's needs and expectations, its fundamental usability was challenged and the overall design language looked outdated. By working on the redesign of the app we were able to reduce user’s confusion with improved readability and app navigation as well as bring new value for our users with new mindfulness features.

* At the time of writing, the Stress Guide app was still under development.
